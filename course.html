Months 1 – 3
Foundations of Vedic Astrology
Build an unshakeable foundation in the cosmic language of Jyotish
Build an unshakeable foundation in the cosmic language of Jyotish. You will learn the philosophical roots, the sacred mathematics, and the essential vocabulary that every Vedic astrologer must master before advancing to prediction.
Learning Objectives
  • Understand the philosophical and historical origins of Jyotish Shastra
  • Learn all 9 planets (Navagrahas) — significations, strengths and weaknesses
  • Master the 12 Rashis (zodiac signs) and their individual characteristics
  • Understand the 12 Bhavas (houses) and what each house governs in life
  • Learn to read and cast a complete Janma Kundali (birth chart)
  • Study the 27 Nakshatras and their profound influence on personality and destiny
  • Understand Panchanga — the five-limbed Vedic calendar and its daily use
Weekly Schedule
Week Topic Content Covered
Week 1–2 Introduction to Jyotish History, philosophy, Vedic worldview, karma and dharma as seen through astrology
Week 3–4 The Navagrahas — 9 Planets Sun to Saturn, Rahu, Ketu — qualities, rulerships, exaltation, debilitation, gem & mantra
Week 5–6 The 12 Rashis Mesha to Meena — elements, modalities, sign lords, body parts ruled, characteristics
Week 7–8 The 12 Bhavas (Houses) Meaning of each house, natural significators, Bhava vs Rashi chart distinctions
Week 9–10 Reading the Birth Chart South Indian & North Indian chart formats, Lagna, planetary positions, basic interpretation
Week 11 The 27 Nakshatras Ashwini to Revati — pada system, deities, animal symbols, compatibility, nakshatra lords
Week 12 Panchanga & Q1 Review Tithi, Vara, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana — Q1 chart reading practice session with Panditji

Months 4 – 6
Intermediate Jyotish — Depth & Prediction
Move into rich predictive tools — Dashas, Vargas, Ashtakavarga
Move beyond basic chart reading into the rich predictive tools of Vedic astrology. Learn how planets interact, how time unfolds through Dasha systems, and how divisional charts reveal deeper dimensions of career, marriage and spiritual life.
Learning Objectives
  • Master planetary aspects (Drishti) — standard and special aspects of Mars, Jupiter, Saturn
  • Understand and identify major Yogas (auspicious combinations) and Doshas
  • Learn the Vimshottari Dasha system — the primary predictive timeline of 120 years
  • Study Divisional Charts (Varga) — Navamsa D-9, Dashamsa D-10, Saptamsa D-7
  • Apply Ashtakavarga for planetary strength analysis and transit predictions
  • Understand planetary dignity — exaltation, debilitation and Moolatrikona positions
  • Conduct intermediate chart readings with event-level predictions
Weekly Schedule
Week Topic Content Covered
Week 13–14 Planetary Aspects (Drishti) 7th house aspect for all planets; special aspects of Mars, Jupiter & Saturn with examples
Week 15–16 Yogas — Auspicious Combos Raja Yogas, Dhana Yogas, Pancha Mahapurusha Yogas, Neecha Bhanga Raja Yoga
Week 17 Doshas & Afflictions Manglik Dosha, Kaal Sarp Dosha, Grahan Dosha — identification and classical remedies
Week 18–19 Vimshottari Dasha System 120-year cycle, Mahadasha, Antardasha, Pratyantara — calculating and applying prediction timelines
Week 20–21 Divisional Charts (Vargas) Navamsa (D-9) for marriage, Dashamsa (D-10) for career, Saptamsa (D-7) for children
Week 22 Ashtakavarga System Sarvashtakavarga, Bhinnashtakavarga, applying Ashtakavarga to transit predictions
Week 23–24 Dignity & Q2 Review Exaltation, debilitation, own sign, Moolatrikona — Shadbala strength — full Q2 prediction workshop
